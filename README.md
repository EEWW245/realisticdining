## 🔪 How to Use

### Chopping Board:
1. **Place the chopping board** - Place it on the ground or workbench
2. **Put ingredients** - Right-click with raw pork, raw chicken, green onion, red pepper, or lettuce
3. **Cut** - Right-click with a knife (sword) multiple times
   - Pork: 25 cuts
   - Chicken: 20 cuts
   - Green onion: 12 cuts
   - Red pepper: 8 cuts
   - Lettuce: 4 cuts
4. **Collect** - Right-click with empty hand to collect chopped ingredients

### Wok Cooking:
1. **Place wok on lit stove** - Must be on a lit Kaleidoscope Cookery stove
2. **Pour oil** - Right-click with oil pot to add vegetable oil
3. **Add ingredients** - Put chopped ingredients into the wok
4. **Stir-fry** - Right-click with spatula 30 times
5. **Wait 40 seconds** - For the dish to cook
6. **Serve** - Right-click with bowl to collect the dish

### Planting System:
1. **Get green onion seeds** - Use shears on grass (45% drop chance)
2. **Plant on farmland** - Right-click on tilled soil
3. **Growth speed** - Same as wheat
